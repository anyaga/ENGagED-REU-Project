    ABOUT

    ENGagED REU was a research experience centered around engineering education. As a part of this program, I learned about different reseach methods including qualitative and quantitative work, I learned about different methods of data analysis such as transcript coding and qualitative data analysis, and I learned how to convey my research findings.

    This process has allowed me to get a greater understanding of problems within the engineering education system and how they effect my journey. While you could say engineering education is nothing more than a curriculm, the research I have come across proves that be a successful engineer, multiple things need to be considered such as the history of the field, the mental health of the participants, the presence of leadership in the field, and more.

    Special thank you to Katreena Thomas and Dr. Brooke Coley for being mentors throught this process.
